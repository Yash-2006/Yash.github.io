MKUltra was an illegal CIA experiment program done during the cold war.

The experimentation was inspired by the experiments done by the Axis powers in world war two where they tried to learn how to control people's minds.

WW2 1953 1964-1967 1973
The Axis powers began unethical experimentation.
It was done in concentration camps.		 The CIA decided to continue this research. The program was starting to get reduced. The program was completely shut down
and many related files were destroyed.
